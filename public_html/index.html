Are you a passionate swim coach looking for an exciting opportunity to lead and inspire a team of dedicated athletes? Do you have a knack for cultivating a positive and supportive environment where swimmers can thrive? Elizabeth Lake swim team in Hampton is seeking a Head Coach and Assistant Coaches for the upcoming summer season!

About Us: Our swim team is located near the Fox Hill are in Hampton. We take pride in our commitment to fostering a welcoming and inclusive environment where swimmers of all ages and abilities can develop their skills and love for the sport. Our team values sportsmanship, camaraderie, and personal growth both in and out of the pool.

Positions Available:

  1. Head Coach:
    • Lead and oversee all aspects of the swim team program
    • Develop and implement training plans to help swimmers reach their full potential
    • Provide technical instruction and feedback to improve stroke technique
    • Coordinate and attend swim meets, practices, and team events
    • Serve as a mentor and role model for athletes and assistant coaches
  2. Assistant Swim Coaches:
    • Assist the Head Coach in planning and executing practices and workouts
    • Provide individualized feedback and support to swimmers during training sessions
    • Help organize and manage swim meets, events, and team activities
    • Foster a positive and encouraging atmosphere for swimmers to learn and grow

Qualifications:

  • Passion for swimming and coaching
  • Previous coaching or competitive swimming experience preferred (for Head Coach position)
  • Strong communication, leadership, and organizational skills
  • Ability to work well with swimmers of all ages and skill levels
  • CPR and lifeguard certification (or willingness to obtain)
